## Important Definitions and Terms
- **Volume Calculation:** Multiplying length, width, and height to find the 3D volume of a block before finding its density.

## Key Points and Lists from the Lecture
- Density = Mass / Volume.
- Pressure = Force / Area.
- Always convert dimensions given in cm into meters before calculating Volume or Area to ensure the final answer is in standard SI units.

## What Students Should Remember
So basically, Unit conversion is the most common pitfall. To convert an Area from cm² to m², you must divide by 10,000 (not just 100). To convert Volume from cm³ to m³, you must divide by 1,000,000.
