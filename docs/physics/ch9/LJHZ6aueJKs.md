# Scientific Method — Class 9 Physics Lecture Summary

## Lecture Overview
This is a core lecture for all sciences. The teacher outlines the Scientific Method, the strict logical process that scientists use to discover new knowledge and validate theories without bias.

## Main Concepts Explained by the Teacher
The teacher walks through the step-by-step procedure of the scientific method. It begins with an Observation, followed by a Hypothesis (an educated guess). Then comes the most crucial step: the Experiment, designed to test the hypothesis. Finally, the scientist analyzes the data to reach a Conclusion, which either supports or rejects the hypothesis.

## Important Definitions and Terms
- **Scientific Method:** A systematic, logical approach to discovering how things in the universe work.
- **Hypothesis:** A proposed explanation made on the basis of limited evidence as a starting point for further investigation.
- **Theory:** A well-substantiated explanation of some aspect of the natural world, based on a body of facts that have been repeatedly confirmed through observation and experiment.

## Key Points and Lists from the Lecture
**Steps of the Scientific Method:**
1. Observation / Asking a Question
2. Formulating a Hypothesis
3. Conducting an Experiment
4. Analyzing Data
5. Drawing a Conclusion
6. Communicating Results

## What Students Should Remember
Look, You guys must memorize the exact sequence of the steps. Furthermore, they must understand the difference between a Hypothesis (a guess before the experiment) and a Theory (a proven concept after many successful experiments).
