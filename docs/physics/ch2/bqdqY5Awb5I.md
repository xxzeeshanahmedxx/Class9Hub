# Kinematics — Class 9 Physics Lecture Summary

## Lecture Overview
This introductory lecture for Chapter 2 focuses on Kinematics, a crucial branch of mechanics. The teacher sets the stage by explaining that we will study how objects move without worrying about why they move (the forces involved).

## Main Concepts Explained by the Teacher
Sir defines mechanics and divides it into Kinematics and Dynamics. Kinematics focuses solely on the description of motion—looking at variables like time, distance, speed, and acceleration. 

## Important Definitions and Terms
- **Mechanics:** The study of motion of objects and the forces acting on them.
- **Kinematics:** The branch of mechanics dealing with the motion of bodies without considering its causes.
- **Dynamics:** The branch of mechanics dealing with the causes of motion (forces).

## Key Points and Lists from the Lecture
- Kinematics focuses purely on "how" things move.
- Dynamics focuses on "why" things move.
- Understanding kinematics is required before studying dynamics in Chapter 3.

## What Students Should Remember
So basically, Always remember that kinematics ignores force and mass. It is purely about describing paths, speeds, and times mathematically.
