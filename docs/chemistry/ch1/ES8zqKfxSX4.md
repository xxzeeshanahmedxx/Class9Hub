## Important Definitions and Terms
- **Chemistry:** The branch of science that deals with the composition, structure, properties, and changes of matter.
- **Matter:** Anything that occupies space and has mass.

## Key Points and Lists from the Lecture
- The syllabus progresses logically from atoms to molecules, then to bulk properties of matter.
- Early chapters focus on physical chemistry and atomic theories.
- Later chapters explore practical applications like environmental chemistry and chemical reactivity.
