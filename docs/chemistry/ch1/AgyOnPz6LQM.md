# What is Chemistry? Branches of Chemistry Part 1 — Class 9 Chemistry Lecture Summary

## Lecture Overview
In this lecture, the teacher officially begins Chapter 1 by deeply answering the question "What is Chemistry?" and introducing the first few branches of this vast scientific field.

## Main Concepts Explained by the Teacher
Our teacher breaks down the definition of chemistry into four pillars: composition (what matter is made of), structure (how it's put together), properties (how it behaves), and reactions (how it changes). Then, he introduces Physical Chemistry and Organic Chemistry, explaining the difference between the physical behavior of matter and the specific study of carbon-containing compounds.

## Important Definitions and Terms
- **Physical Chemistry:** The branch dealing with the relationship between the physical properties of substances and their chemical composition.
- **Organic Chemistry:** The study of covalent compounds of carbon and hydrogen (hydrocarbons) and their derivatives.

## Key Points and Lists from the Lecture
- Matter is everything around us.
- Physical chemistry covers topics like the structure of atoms, the behavior of gases, and the effect of temperature.
- Organic chemistry is the basis of life and covers things like petroleum, plastics, and pharmaceuticals.

## What Students Should Remember
Listen carefully here: Students really need to memorize the exact definition of organic chemistry. it is specifically the study of hydrocarbons and their derivatives. confusing it with inorganic chemistry is a very common mistake in board exams.
