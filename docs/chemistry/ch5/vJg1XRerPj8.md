## Important Definitions and Terms
- **Conceptual Thermodynamics:** Applying the rules of heat flow to solve theoretical chemistry questions.

## Key Points and Lists from the Lecture
- Reviewing that Exothermic = Negative $\Delta H$, Endothermic = Positive $\Delta H$.
- Confirming that breaking bonds requires energy (endothermic process).
- Reviewing the aerobic respiration equation as a prime example of an exothermic reaction.

## What Students Should Remember
Actually, the most common error reviewed here is confusing the energy hill diagrams. Make absolutely sure you know that in an Exothermic diagram, the Product line is drawn significantly LOWER than the Reactant line, because energy was lost to the surroundings.
