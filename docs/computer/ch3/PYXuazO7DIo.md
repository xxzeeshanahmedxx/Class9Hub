# Real world Applications of Computer Networks — Class 9 Computer Science Lecture Summary

## Lecture Overview
In this final video for Chapter 3, the teacher summarizes the Real-World Applications of Computer Networks. This lecture contextualizes all the previous theories, showing how networks actively shape modern human society.

## Main Concepts Explained by the Teacher
The instructor highlights several industries revolutionized by networking. E-commerce (buying and selling online), E-learning (online classes and research), and Telemedicine (remote surgeries and diagnostics) are discussed in detail. The teacher emphasizes how networks have transformed the world into a "Global Village," where physical distance no longer prevents instant communication or business transactions.

## Important Definitions and Terms
- **E-commerce:** The buying and selling of goods or services using the internet.
- **Global Village:** The idea that the entire world has become more interconnected as the result of the propagation of media technologies and the internet.

## Key Points and Lists from the Lecture
- Video conferencing allows global business meetings without travel.
- ATM networks and online banking allow instantaneous, global financial transfers.
- Cloud computing allows users to store data on remote servers instead of local hard drives.

## What Students Should Remember
