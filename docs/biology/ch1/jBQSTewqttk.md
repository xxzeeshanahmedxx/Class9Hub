# Biology and Its Branches — Class 9 Biology Lecture Summary

## Lecture Overview
In this lecture, teh teacher officially begins Chapter 1 by dividing the massive subject of Biology into specific, manageable Branches. This helps students understand the specialized fields within biological sciences.

## Main Concepts Explained by the Teacher
The instructor divides biology into three main divisions: Zoology (animals), Botany (plants), and Microbiology (microorganisms). From there, Sir explains specific branches like Morphology (physical structure), Anatomy (internal structure), Histology (tissues), Cell Biology, Physiology (functions), Genetics (inheritance), and Taxonomy (classification).

## Important Definitions and Terms
- **Morphology:** The study of the form and structure of living organisms.
- **Physiology:** The study of the normal functions of living organisms and their parts.
- **Genetics:** The study of genes, genetic variation, and heredity in organisms.

## Key Points and Lists from the Lecture
- **Botany:** Focuses on flora (plants).
- **Zoology:** Focuses on fauna (animals).
- **Entomology:** Study of insects.
- **Immunology:** Study of the immune system.

## What Students Should Remember
Honestly, matching the branch to its definition is the most common MCQ in this chapter. Do not confuse Morphology (external structure) with Anatomy (internal structure).
